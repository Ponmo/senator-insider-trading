112th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 29

To permit the waiving of the reading of an amendment if the text and adequate notice are provided.
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 27, 2011
Mr. Udall of Colorado (for himself and Mr. Merkley) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
To permit the waiving of the reading of an amendment if the text and adequate notice are provided.
Resolved,
SECTION 1. READING OF AMENDMENTS.
(a) Standing Order.—This section shall be a standing order of the Senate.
(b) Waiver.—The reading of an amendment may be waived by a non-debatable motion if the amendment—
(1) has been submitted at least 72 hours before the motion; and
(2) is available in printed or electronic form in the Congressional Record.
