JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the final rule of the Bureau of Land Management relating to “Waste Prevention, Production Subject to Royalties, and Resource Conservation”.
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Land Management relating to “Waste Prevention, Production Subject to Royalties, and Resource Conservation” (published at 81 Fed. Reg. 83008 (November 18, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
Passed the House of Representatives February 3, 2017.
