Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That the issuance of a license amendment to export with respect to the following proposed export to Israel is prohibited:
(1) The export of the following defense articles, defense services, and technical data described in Transmittal No. DDTC 23â€“100, which was submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(c) of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776(c)) and communication of which was published in the Congressional Record on September 18, 2024 (Executive Communication 5937): defense articles, defense services, and technical data to support procurement of Boeing Direct Attack Weapons (Joint Direct Attack Munition variants and Small Diameter Bomb Increment I variants).
