AN ACT
To protect the retired pay of certain members of the Armed Forces convicted
of an offense under section 2071(b) of title 18, United States Code.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. MEANING OF THE TERM `OFFICE' IN THE CASE OF CERTAIN MEMBERS OF
THE ARMED FORCES
  (a) IN GENERAL- Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no person who
  is a retired regular officer of the
Armed Forces of the United States shall be considered as holding an office
of the United States for purposes of section 2071(b) of title 18, United
States Code.
  (b) EFFECTIVE DATE- This Act shall take effect as of January 1, 1989.
Passed the Senate November 2 (legislative day, September 18), 1989.
