AN ACT
To prevent the desecration of the United States flag.
  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United
  States of America in Congress assembled, That section 700(a) of title 18,
  United States Code is amended by inserting in subsection (a), following
  the word `burning,' the words `displaying the flag of the United States
  on the floor or ground'.
Passed the Senate March 16 (legislative day, January 3), 1989.
