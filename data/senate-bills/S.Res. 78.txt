IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

Mr. HOLLINGS (for himself, Mr. BYRD, Mr. FORD, Mr. CONRAD, Mr. HELMS,
Mr. THURMOND, Mr. INOUYE, Mr. HEFLIN, Mr. SANFORD, Mr. AKAKA, Mr. WELLSTONE,

RESOLUTION
To disapprove the request of the President for extension of the fast track
procedures under the Omnibus Trade and Competitiveness Act of 1988 and the
Trade Act of 1974.
  Resolved, That the Senate disapproved the request of the President for
  the extension, under section 1103(b)(1)(B)(i) of the Omnibus Trade and
  Competitiveness Act of 1988, of the provisions of section 151 of the Trade
  Act of 1974 to any implementing bill submitted with respect to any trade
  agreement entered into under section 1102 (b) or (c) of such Act after
  May 31, 1991, because sufficient tangible progress has not been made in
  trade negotiations.
